About us

AgroTech Astana IT University

At AgroTech Astana IT University, we are dedicated to transforming modern agriculture through smart technology and innovation. Our mission is to empower growers, researchers, and agricultural businesses with intelligent tools that improve efficiency, sustainability, and crop quality.

We combine advanced technologies such as Internet of Things (IoT), data analytics, and artificial intelligence to provide real-time monitoring and smart management of agricultural environments. Through our mobile application and web platform, users can easily track sensor data, manage devices, and gain valuable insights to support better decision-making.

Beyond technology, AgroTech also connects innovation with real-world products. Through our integrated store, we offer high-quality microgreens, fresh flowers, and hydroponic plants, making it easier for customers to access sustainable and locally grown products.

Our platform is designed to simplify complex processes while delivering a seamless and user-friendly experience across devices. Whether you are managing crop production, analyzing plant health, or purchasing fresh agricultural products, AgroTech provides a complete ecosystem in one place.

Developed within the innovative environment of Astana IT University, AgroTech reflects a strong collaboration between students, researchers, and industry needs. We are committed to continuous improvement, research-driven development, and creating practical solutions that contribute to the future of smart agriculture.
